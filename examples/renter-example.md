# Renter Example

This fictional example shows how a renter might prepare facts before asking an AI assistant to explain legal information. It is not legal advice.

## Profile

- Name or alias: Renter
- Country: Australia
- State or territory: Queensland
- Council or local government area: Brisbane
- Purpose: Household
- Privacy level: Private

## Legal Surfaces

- Residential tenancy agreement.
- Bond and rent records.
- Repairs, entry notices and communication history.
- Household pets and noise.
- Local services or complaint pathways.

## Evidence Checklist

- [ ] Tenancy agreement.
- [ ] Bond lodgement details.
- [ ] Photos, dates and repair requests.
- [ ] Emails, texts or notices.
- [ ] Date the official tenancy guidance was checked.

## Plain-English Questions To Explore

- What official tenancy source explains the issue?
- What facts are missing?
- Is the decision high risk enough to contact a tenancy advice service, official source or lawyer?
