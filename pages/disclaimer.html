Plain-English disclaimer

Legal Memory Workbench is a preparation tool. It helps organise facts, questions and source lists. It does not answer legal problems for visitors.

What this site is

This website is for legal information preparation only. It helps visitors create structured markdown files for their own AI workflow.

What this site is not

  • It does not provide legal advice.
  • It does not create a lawyer-client relationship.
  • It does not check whether a legal source is current, complete or correct.
  • It does not send questions to a lawyer, court, regulator, AI provider or government agency.

Important limits

  • Laws change.
  • AI systems can make mistakes.
  • Visitors should check official sources directly.
  • High-risk decisions require qualified legal advice.

Privacy and storage

No data is transmitted by this static site. Form entries are saved only in the visitor's browser through localStorage until the visitor clears them or the browser removes them. Downloaded markdown files stay wherever the visitor saves them.
