Partner components

Legal Memory Workbench can sit beside other Strange But True builders as one practical layer in a wholesome digital twin scaffold.

The important boundary: these components can be connected by the visitor, but this site does not merge, upload or publish their data.

Plain boundary

A visitor can choose to keep these files separate, combine them in their own private folder, or copy selected parts into their own AI workflow. Legal Memory Workbench does not publish partner-component data and does not claim that a combined scaffold is legally complete.
